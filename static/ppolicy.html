Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MediMate collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following data when you use MediMate:

  • Birthdate and gender
  • Medical history and wellness inputs

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Monitor wellness trends
  • Improve user experience

3. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement encryption and access control to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties unless legally required.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to view, modify, or delete your data at any time. Please contact our support team for assistance.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Contact
